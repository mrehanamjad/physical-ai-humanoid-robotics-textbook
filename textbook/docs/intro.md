# Welcome to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook

This AI-native textbook is designed to teach the principles of physical artificial intelligence and humanoid robotics.

## Modules

This textbook is organized into four modules:

1.  **The Robotic Nervous System (ROS 2)**: Learn the fundamentals of robot middleware and control.
2.  **The Digital Twin (Gazebo & Unity)**: Dive into simulation, physics, and virtual environments.
3.  **The AI–Robot Brain (NVIDIA Isaac)**: Explore perception, navigation, and learning.
4.  **Vision–Language–Action (VLA) & Humanoid Intelligence**: Understand cognition, interaction, and autonomy.
