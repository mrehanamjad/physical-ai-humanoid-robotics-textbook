# Introduction

Welcome to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook.

This open-source textbook is designed to provide a comprehensive and practical introduction to the exciting world of modern robotics and artificial intelligence. Whether you are a student, a hobbyist, or a professional looking to expand your skills, this book will guide you through the fundamental concepts and hands-on projects needed to build and program intelligent robots.

## What you'll learn

Throughout this book, you will learn about:
- The core principles of Physical AI and embodied intelligence.
- The Robot Operating System (ROS 2) for building robust robot software.
- Creating digital twins and simulations using Gazebo.
- NVIDIA's Isaac Sim for advanced robotics simulation.
- Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models for creating intelligent robot brains.
