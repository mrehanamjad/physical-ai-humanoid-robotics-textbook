# Sim-to-Real Transfer

## Overview

This chapter focuses on the challenges and techniques for transferring control policies trained in simulation to a physical robot.

## Learning Objectives

- Understand the sim-to-real gap.
- Techniques for domain randomization.
- Strategies for successful sim-to-real transfer.
