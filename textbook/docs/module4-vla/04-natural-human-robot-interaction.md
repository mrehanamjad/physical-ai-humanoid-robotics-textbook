# Natural Human-Robot Interaction

## Overview

This chapter focuses on designing and implementing natural and intuitive ways for humans to interact with humanoid robots.

## Learning Objectives

- Understand the principles of Human-Robot Interaction (HRI).
- Learn about gesture recognition and natural language understanding.
- Design a simple conversational interaction with a robot.
