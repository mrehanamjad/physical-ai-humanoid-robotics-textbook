# Humanoid Robot Kinematics and Dynamics

## Overview

This chapter covers the fundamental concepts of humanoid robot kinematics and dynamics.

## Learning Objectives

- Understand forward and inverse kinematics for humanoid robots.
- Learn about the dynamics of bipedal locomotion.
- Analyze the forces and torques involved in humanoid robot movement.
